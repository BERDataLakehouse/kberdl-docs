# Data Dictionary

This page lists all registered tenants in the KBase BER Data Lakehouse (K-BERDL), along with their descriptions, organizations, namespace prefixes, and available databases.

??? info "AI ALE"
    **Organization:** ANL

    **Namespace prefix:** `aiale_`

    **Description:** Database for AI-driven adaptive lab evolution of ADP1. This database holds observed mutations crossed with conditions, passages, and strains where the mutations are observed. Key mutations are linked to specific phenotypes like growth with a novel DAHP biosynthesis enzyme and growth on a range of methoxylated aromatic compounds.

    **Databases (1):**

    - `aiale_dataset1`
